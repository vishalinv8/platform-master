Plai Today. Download, connect, and get active!

Plai Today is the new Social Active Lifestyle App that connects you to people you like for the activities you love. Forget multiple apps for getting together. Plai Today is a FREE all in one solution for activity meet ups happening today. Say hello to the world, with Plai Today.

Don't just Plai. Plai With Friends!

Studies have shown that staying socially active has extensive health and wellness benefits above and beyond those of solitary exercise. Improved quality of life, more personal motivation, and long-term health benefits are just a few of the reasons people stay active with friends. It's just more fun!

Features

  • Public and Private Events

    Find a quick pick-up game after work, or schedule a private scrimage.

  • Search Feature

    Find events, friends, venues, and the activities that you are interested in.

  • Messages / Comments

    Post questions or notes to the attendees of an event.

  • Find the Best Venues

    Powered by FourSquare™. See our Featured Locations for commercial facilities and event hosting.

  • Add Event Photos

    See, and be seen. Take a photo and instantly upload it for others.

  • Manage Your Profile

    You can optionally publish your activity profile (and photo).

Plai Today Key Highlights

International

Plai Today works anywhere in the world. It's great for staying active while traveling, or keeping it local.

Cross-Platform

Plai Today runs on both iOS and Android.

Quick and Easy

It's never been easier to find, join, or publish events, whether to a private group of people, or to a wider enthusiast community.

Powered by The Cloud

Our backend is completely virtualized and deployed on best-in-class data technologies.

Get Started Today

Plai Today is completely free. Sign up and get active in your community. Invite some friends to come Plai, or make new friends based on shared interests and activity profiles.

  • Sign in with your Google™ or Facebook™ account
  • Find activities you are interested in, or create your own.
  • Invite your friends to Plai Today!
